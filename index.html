About Us

The True Technology Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to expanding access to robotics, artificial intelligence, and emerging technology education. We operate through three interconnected pillars: a research lab that develops and tests new technology, structured STEM programs in K–12 schools, and competitions and events that motivate students and showcase innovation.

The foundation designs and manages its programs, selecting partners and vendors based on what best serves students and teachers. Our role is to ensure every program advances educational goals and creates meaningful opportunities for young people in technology.

Our Programs

Robotics & AI Research Lab

Our research lab focuses on robotics, artificial intelligence, and emerging technology. Work includes testing new robotics systems, studying how AI can improve learning, developing open technology tools, and running pilot projects with schools and industry partners.

STEM Events & Competitions

We host robotics competitions, technology challenges, hackathons, and innovation events that bring students together to build robots, solve engineering problems, and explore careers in robotics and AI.

STEM Technology for Education

We partner with K–12 schools to introduce structured robotics and AI programs in classrooms, providing equipment, curriculum, teacher training, and ongoing program support to help students develop real technical skills.
